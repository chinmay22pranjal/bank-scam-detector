AI-based Bank Scam Detection

This system helps a bank detect fraudulent or suspicious online transactions before the money leaves the customer’s account. It uses a risk-scoring model based on real banking parameters like amount, device trust, location change and transaction time.

Why is this important?

  • Digital payments and UPI are increasing, so online scams are also increasing.
  • Manual monitoring is slow; AI can flag risky transactions instantly.
  • Early detection helps reduce financial loss for customers and banks.

How this detection works

  • High amount + new device + unusual location = higher fraud score.
  • Night-time transactions are slightly more risky than day-time transactions.
  • If the account has previous fraud history, risk goes up.
  • All these signals are combined into a 0–100% risk score.
